Information about the website

Welcome to MeetMyCulture! or also called MMC! This website helps you find cultures you can explore or learn! its also helpful in research work and school work. MMC is a non-profitable website which is an online platform which helps users express their culture or help others explore other cultures and accepts and is open for anyone.
